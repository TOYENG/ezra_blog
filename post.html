The greatest pleasure in life is doing what people say you cannot do.

Posted by brandongaille on 09/23/13

What It Means Basically, what this quote means is that the best feeling in life can be attained through showing people that said you couldn't, that you actually could. Proving people wrong and not limiting yourself by what others say you should and can or cannot do. You should define yourself and when ever someone tells you that you aren't capable of doing something, show them that you can do it and that you can even do it better. Showing people that you are capable of doing what they think you can't is better than any revenge because you showed them that they were wrong, and you may have even gained a few things in the process.
